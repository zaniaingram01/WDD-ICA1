Our History....

AJ's Little Shop was founded in 2020. Something which started off as a quarintine activity as wel as a way to pay off student bills has turned into a beaded jewellry community which is loved by many. In the beginning we only sold to locals here in sunny South Africa. However now we want the world to experiance all the love which goes into creating your own piece of beaded jewelery as well as experiancing the journey that goes along with it.
